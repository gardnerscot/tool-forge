Our Story

From cornfield to copper still.

We grow our own grain on 80 acres of Adams County farmland. Every bottle of Red Barn bourbon starts as seed in our soil. We mill, mash, ferment, and distill on-site in a restored 1902 dairy barn.

No shortcuts. No sourced whiskey. No neutral grain spirits. Just grain, water, yeast, oak, and time.

— The Miller Family

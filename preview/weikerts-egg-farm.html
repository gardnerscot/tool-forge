Our Story

Nine generations on this land.

The Weikert family has been farming this land since 1834 — that's nine generations of sunrises, soil, and stewardship. We raise our hens free-walking (never caged) and our cattle naturally fed, without hormones, antibiotics, or shortcuts.

Our farm store is open daily. Come see where your food comes from — the eggs, the beef, and yes, the beef bacon made from brisket that everyone's talking about.

— The Weikert Family

Our Story

Know your farmer. Love your food.

Farmstead Boxes was born from a simple idea: the best meat comes from people you know. We partner with local farms across Adams County and Central Pennsylvania to bring you pasture-raised beef, pork, and chicken — plus handmade cheeses, jams, and more.

Every box is packed frozen, delivered in a recyclable cooler, and guaranteed for 12 hours from drop-off. Free delivery Wednesdays to Adams County and Hanover.

— Stan & the Farmstead Family

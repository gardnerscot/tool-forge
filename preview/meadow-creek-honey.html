Meet the Maker

Sixteen hives. One family. Pure honey.

My grandfather kept bees. My father kept bees. Now my daughter and I tend sixteen hives across three meadows in Adams County. Every jar is raw, unfiltered, and harvested by hand.

We never heat our honey, never blend it, and never rush the bees. They do the work. We just give them a good home.

— Tom Weaver, Beekeeper

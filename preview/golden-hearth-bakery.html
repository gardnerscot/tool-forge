Our Story

3 AM. Flour. Water. Salt. Magic.

Since 2012, our bakers have been arriving before dawn to hand-shape every loaf, croissant, and pastry. We use organic flour, local butter, and a 14-year-old sourdough starter named Beatrice.

Nothing frozen. Nothing commercial. Just flour, water, salt, and patience — the way bread was meant to be made.

— Rachel & the Golden Hearth team

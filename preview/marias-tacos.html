Our Story

Three generations. One recipe.

Maria opened this kitchen in 1994 with her grandmother's recipes and a single burner. Thirty years later, we're still making every tortilla by hand and slow-cooking our carnitas the way Abuela taught us — no shortcuts, no compromises.

We source our chiles from Oaxaca, our limes from local farms, and our energy from the families who've been eating here since day one.

— Maria Hernandez, Owner

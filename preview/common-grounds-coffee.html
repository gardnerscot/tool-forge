Our Story

A place where everyone knows your roast.

Common Grounds opened in 2016 with a Chemex, a refurbished La Marzocco, and a belief that great coffee brings people together. We roast our beans weekly in a 5-kilo roaster right behind the bar.

Single-origin, direct-trade beans. Pour-overs, espresso, and house-made syrups. Open mic Wednesdays. Free Wi-Fi. Dogs welcome on the patio.

— Jess & the Common Grounds crew
